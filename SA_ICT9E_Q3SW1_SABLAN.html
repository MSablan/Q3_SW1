Saving Private Ryan: Information about characters


Summary:

After the D-Day landings on June 6, 1944. Captain Miller is tasked to find a Private, James Ryan. Reason is because all three brothers of Private cryan have been killed in action. Unfortunately, Captain Miller and most of the men he took with him were killed, however Private Ryan managed to return home safely.

Captain John H. Miller

The main character in this movie, Captain John H. Miller leads 8 men on a mission to find Private James Ryan after the D-Day landings. Unfortunately he loses a few men on the way. His final battle was during the battle of Ramelle where he dies after defending the town of Ramelle with the last few men he has and Private James Ryan.

Sergeant Michael "Mike" Horvath

Close friend of Cpt Miller, Sgt Horvath served with Cpt Miller in Africa, and in Italy before the invasion of France. Before the mission, he was picked by Cpt Miller to be his second-in-command during this mission. However he as well would be killed during the battle for Ramelle.

Private First Class Richard Reiben

PFC Richard Reiben, one of the men chosen by Cpt Miller for the mission. Not much is known about him, but during the mission, it was shown in the movie his disliking to the mission. During the battle of Ramelle, he was one of the only survivors of the battle, he showed great sadness to the death of Cpt Miller at the end of the film.

Timothy E. Upham

Timothy Upham was a technician before the mission, Upham is basically seen as a "coward" and someone who is new to the fighting. His character though symbolizes the loss of innocence during wartime. At the end of the film, he manages to kill the soldier who wounded and killed his Captain.

Private Daniel Jackson

The left-handed marksman of his team, Private Jackson served in the landings at Omaha Beach during D-Day and was chosen by Cpt Miller to be in the team. Unfortunately during the battle for Ramelle, he was killed when an enemy tank fired at his position.

Private Stanley Mellish

Private Mellish Accompanied Cpt Miller and the others during the landings at Omaha beach. He was also selected to be in the team to search for Private Ryan. During the battle for Ramelle, he was chosen to be the Assistant Machine Gunner of one of the soldiers already at Ramelle. Unfortunately during the battle, he was killed brutally by an enemy soldier.
